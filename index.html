Welcome

Quick instructions on how to use respawn.bet.

1. Connect Wallet

Hit the Connect button in the header to link your wallet. You’ll need it to respawn tokens, bet on streams, and track all your activity in one place.

2. Discover Action
  • Whether it’s a token revival or a live stream, finding the next big play is easy:
  • Use Search to filter and Sort to reorder the list.
  • Click a token or stream to see quick stats and live activity.
  • Feeling lucky? Hit Random Bet and let fate choose a token or stream for you to jump into.
3. Respawn, Bet & Watch
  • Open the token or stream’s action panel to enter your amount and get a live quote.
  • Confirm the transaction and watch it unfold in real-time, complete with live updates and stream reactions.
  • Track all your active bets and revivals in the Revivals section below — perfect for both streamers and viewers to follow the action.
  • Build hype by sharing your stream or revival link and let others bet on the chaos while you go live.
